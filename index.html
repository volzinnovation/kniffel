How to Play Yahtzee

üéØ Objective

Score the most points by rolling dice and filling in your scorecard.

üé≤ Gameplay

  • Each turn, roll up to 3 times
  • Click dice to hold them between rolls
  • After rolling, select a category to score
  • Each category can only be used once

üìä Upper Section

  • Ones - Sixes: Sum of matching dice
  • Bonus: +35 if upper section totals 63+

üìä Lower Section

  • Three of a Kind: Sum of all dice (need 3 matching)
  • Four of a Kind: Sum of all dice (need 4 matching)
  • Full House: 25 points (3 of one + 2 of another)
  • Small Straight: 30 points (4 consecutive dice)
  • Large Straight: 40 points (5 consecutive dice)
  • Yahtzee: 50 points (5 of a kind)
  • Chance: Sum of all dice

‚≠ê Yahtzee Bonus

If you roll additional Yahtzees after scoring 50 in Yahtzee, you get +100 bonus points each!
